Our Story

Trusted Property Investment Partner Since 2020

Redmarb Realtors Kenya offers a comprehensive spectrum of property management, maintenance, and consultancy services. With offices in Ngong Rd and Ruaka Hill Road, Nairobi, the company predominantly serves Rosslyn and its environs. Founded in 2020, Redmarb boasts a diverse portfolio spanning residential, commercial, and mixed-use properties.

As a trusted property investment partner in Kenya, our presence extends beyond borders, embracing development opportunities abroad. Renowned for our professionalism and unwavering commitment to quality, we have established ourselves as exceptional residential property managers, delivering peace of mind and maintaining the integrity of client and property owner relationships.

Located at Amani Orchid Apartments, QQQP+6X6, Ruaka Hill Rd, Nairobi, our dedicated team brings expertise, integrity, and personalized service to every transaction. We are committed to excellence and client satisfaction, making us a trusted name in the Kenyan real estate market.
